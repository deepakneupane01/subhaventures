About Subha Ventures

Building Nepal's Next
Great Institution

Originally incorporated as Jagatra Devi Investment Private Limited, Subha Ventures Limited has undergone a strategic transformation — increasing authorized capital to NPR 50 million and adopting a broader, more ambitious corporate identity.

The company is now preparing for conversion into a Public Limited Company, targeting paid-up capital of NPR 500 million (50 Crore), expanding to approximately 150 long-term, committed shareholders.

To become a leading Nepali public investment and management company that drives sustainable economic growth, fosters innovation, and delivers long-term value to shareholders, partners, and society.

  • Invest responsibly in high-growth, future-ready sectors of Nepal's economy.
  • Build professionally managed businesses with strong governance structures.
  • Maximize shareholder value through ethical practices, innovation, and operational excellence.
  • Contribute meaningfully to Nepal's economic development and employment generation.

Integrity

Transparency, accountability, and ethical conduct in all business activities.

Professionalism

Decisions driven by expertise, data, and long-term strategic thinking.

Sustainability

Long-term value creation prioritized over short-term speculative gains.

Innovation

Embracing technology and new business models to stay competitive.

Shareholder Alignment

Protecting and enhancing shareholder interests at every stage of growth.
